Instructions

  1. Adding a new battery
    • Click the green "Add Battery" button at the top of the page
    • In the popup, give your battery the following information:
      • Nickname
      • Brand (or description)
      • Select whether the battery is a deep cycle
      • Enter the rated amp hours (deep cycle) or CCA rating (non deep cycle)
      • Select a colour (color for our imperial friends ðŸ˜‰)
    • Click the green "Add" button
  2. Updating a battery
    • On the battery card, click the "Edit / Add Reading" button
    • Change any of the battery information
    • Click the green "Update" button
  3. Delete a battery - NOTE: This will delete all readings for this battery and cannot be undone
    • Click the "X" in the battery card's top right corner
    • Click "Ok" in the confirmation dialog to confirm, or "Cancel" to abort
  4. Add a battery voltage reading
    • On the appropriate battery card, click the "Edit / Add Reading" button
    • In the popup, click the blue "Add Reading" button
    • A new popup will appear where you can enter the voltage reading (E.g. "12.45")
    • Click the "Add" button to add the reading, or cancel to abort.
      You should now see your voltage reading on the graph as well as in the voltage readings table.
  5. Change a voltage reading
    If you have entered an incorrect voltage, you can update the reading
    • In the voltage readings table, click the "Edit" button
    • In the prompt, enter a new voltage reading value to replace the current one
    • Click "Ok" to confirm the changes or "Cancel" to abort
  6. Delete a voltage reading
    • In the voltage readings table, click the "Delete" button relative to the reading you wish to delete
    • In the confirmation prompt, click "Ok" to confirm deletion or "Cancel" to abort
